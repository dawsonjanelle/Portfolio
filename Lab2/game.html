Bobby's House

Welcome to the Glowing Village, this is Bobby's house! In this village there are myths of a Princess named Adrienne Victorine. It is said that a witch stuck her up in a tower 12 years ago, and she hasn't been seen since. Bobby's life long dream is to be able to become a knight and join the ranks to save the princess. The only problem with that though is to join the ranks Bobby would need to be 18 and he is only 10. He is about to venture out on his own to find the missing princess, but not without your help! Guide Bobby through his journey to find Princess Adrienne Victorine! Choose where you go wisely, because you may fall into a trap!

Janelle Dawson
